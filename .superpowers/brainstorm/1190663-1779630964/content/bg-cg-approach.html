Background & CG Rendering

How to handle background images and fullscreen CG/event images

Backgrounds (SetBg)

BG images are 1280x720 JPG files (bg_XXXX.jpg). Only one active background at a time.

A

Single ImageNode entity

One entity with ImageNode. SetBg swaps the texture. Simple, one entity to manage.

B

Dual-buffer for transitions

Two ImageNode entities. Cross-fade between old and new bg by lerping alpha. Enables smooth transitions.

CG / Event Images (ShowCg)

CG images are 1280x720 RGBA PNG (eve_XXXXXX.png). Fullscreen overlay, hides sprites and dialogue.

A

Overlay entity

Spawn CG entity on ShowCg (on top of bg, covering sprites). Despawn on HideCg. Separate from bg system.

B

Reuse bg entity

ShowCg replaces the bg texture. Simpler but mixes concerns.

Transition handling

ScriptCmd has transition: Option<Transition> on SetBg/ShowFg/HideFg. For Phase 3, should we implement:

A

Fade transitions everywhere

Fade in/out for bg changes, sprite show/hide, CG show/hide. Duration from script or default 500ms.

B

Instant first, transitions later

Phase 3 = instant swaps. Transitions as a separate Phase 3.1 or Phase 4 task.

C

Fade for BG/CG only

Instant sprite swaps, fade transitions for background/CG changes. Balanced approach.
